Our Story

Evergreen Threads was founded with the belief that fashion can be sustainable and ethical. We use organic materials and eco-friendly manufacturing practices to create clothing that is kind to the planet and comfortable to wear.

We are committed to transparency and fair labor practices. We partner with small, family-owned workshops that ensure their workers are treated with respect and paid a living wage.
